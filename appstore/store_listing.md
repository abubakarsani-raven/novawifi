## App Review Information — Notes (paste this)
Nova Heronix WiFi Manager writes Wi‑Fi credentials to the Nova Cube (an NFC device) and to standard NTAG21x NFC tags. NFC hardware cannot be exercised on the Simulator or on a device without NFC, so a demo video is provided.

Demo video: <PASTE UNLISTED VIDEO LINK>

The video was filmed on a physical iPhone and shows, in frame with the Nova Cube hardware:
1. Pairing: writing a Wi‑Fi network to the Nova Cube over NFC (the "Hold near tag" flow).
2. Reading it back / launching the one‑tap App Clip join by tapping the Cube.
3. The full workflow: manage networks, write, lock a tag, and the guest QR card.

No account or login is required. The Nova Cube (or any NTAG21x tag) is required to exercise NFC write/read.
